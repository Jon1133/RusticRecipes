Rustic Mains!

Beef & Vegitable Stew (Serves 6)

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp of Oil
  • 900g Lean Beef Stew Meat, Cubed
  • 5 Shallots, Finely Chopped
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic, Minced
  • 60ml of Canned Tomato Paste
  • 240ml of Red Wine
  • 1200ml of Water
  • 2 Bay Leaves
  • 450g of Cubed Potatoes
  • 450g of Cubed Carrots
  • 450g of Cubed Celery Root
  • 230g of Frozen Peas
  • Salt to Taste

Method:

  1. Heat a large pot over medium-high heat and add half of the oil.
  2. Once hot add half of the beef and brown it on all sides.
  3. Remove beef to a plate and finish with the other half of the beef, using the other half of the oil.
  4. Once the beef is finished, put all the meat back into the pot and add the shallots and garlic. Cook until translucent, about 5 minutes.
  5. Add the tomato paste and roast until fragrant, stirring often.
  6. Deglaze the pan by stirring in the red wine and cook until the alcohol has evaporated.
  7. Add the water, bay leaves and potatoes then season with salt and pepper.
  8. Bring to a boil, reduce to a simmer and cook for 45 minutes.
  9. Add the carrots and celeriac to the pot and continue simmering for 20 minutes.
  10. Add the peas and finish cooking for 10 minutes or until the meat is very tender.
  11. Check for seasoning and add salt if needed.
  12. Serve with crusty bread.

Hunters Chicken (Serves 2)

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp of Vegetable Oil (Plus extra for the tray)
  • 2 Skinless Chicken Breasts
  • 4 Rashers of Smoked Back Bacon
  • 1 Red Onion, Sliced
  • 50ml of Sticky BBQ Sauce
  • 25g of Grated Parmesan

Method:

  1. Heat oven to 180°C/200°C. Grease a tray with oil, place chicken breasts on tray and cover each breast with 2 bacon rashers. Place in the oven for 20-25mins. Until cooked through.
  2. While chicken is cooking, fry the onion in 1tbsp olive oil until golden brown and soft, then set aside.
  3. Put the potatoes into a large pan of boiling water and cook for 10-15mins until soft.
  4. Put another smaller pan of water on the hob and bring to the boil while the potatoes are cooking. Once boiling, tip in the runner beans and carrots to cook for approx 8-10mins.
  5. Drain the potatoes and return to the pan with the milk and cream cheese. Mash until smooth and stir through the fried onions.
  6. Once the chicken is cooked, pour over the BBQ sauce and parmesan and return to the oven for 2 mins until the cheese has melted. Serve with the mash, carrots and runner beans.

Mushrooms Stroganoff(Serves 2)

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp of Oil
  • 1 Medium Onion, Diced
  • 4 Cloves of Garlic, Minced
  • 500g mushrooms, Sliced or Diced
  • 1 tsp of Smoked Paprika
  • 70ml of Vegitable Stock
  • 2 tbsp of Sour Cream
  • Salt & Black Pepper
  • 4 tbsp of Fresh Parsley, Chopped

Method:

  1. Heat the oil in a large frying pan or wok, add the onion and cook for a few minutes over a medium heat, until slightly softened.
  2. Add the garlic and mushrooms, and cook for a further 5 minutes or until the mushrooms are thoroughly cooked.
  3. Coat the mushrooms in the smoked paprika then add the vegetable stock and sour cream.
  4. Bring to a gente simmer and cook until the mixture has come together into a silky sauce.
  5. Season to taste and serve topped with plenty of fresh parsley.
