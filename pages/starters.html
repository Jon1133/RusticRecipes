Tomato Soup (Serves 4)

Ingredients:

  • 1 Large Onion, Diced
  • 1 Carrot, Finely Chopped
  • 1 Celery Stick, Finely Chopped
  • 50g of Butter
  • Tyme
  • 2 Bay Leaves
  • 500g of Ripe Plum or Vine Tomatoes, Roughly Chopped
  • 850ml of Chicken Stock
  • 8 tbsp of Creme Fraiche
  • Basil Leaves To Taste

Method:

  1. Cook the onion, carrot and celery in the butter for 10-15 minutes until soft.
  2. Add the thyme, bay leaves, and cook for a minute.
  3. Tip in the tomatoes and chicken stock.
  4. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Fish out the herbs, add 4 tbsp of the creme fraiche then use a stick blender or food processor to whisk until completely smooth.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste, then ladle into bowls adding another spoon of creme fraiche and a few basil leaves to each bowl.

Prawn Cocktail (Serves 4)

Ingredients:

  • 75g of Light Mayonnaise
  • 20g of Ketchup
  • A Dash of Worcestershire Sauce
  • 200g of Cooked Prawns
  • A Spinkle of Paprika
  • Lemon Slices

Method:

  1. Measure out the mayonnaise, ketchup and worcestershire sauce and mix them together in a bowl.
  2. Add the prawns and mix until completely coated. Cover and place in the fridge until ready to serve.
  3. To serve, place spoonfuls of the prawn cocktail mixture onto lettuce leaves or use as a healthy baked potato topping.
  4. Sprinkle over a little paprika and serve with fresh lemon slices on the side.

Stuffed Mushrooms (Serves 2)

Ingredients:

  • 4-6 Large Portobello Mushrooms
  • 125g of Unsmoked Bacon Lardons
  • 1 Shallot, Finely Chopped
  • 1 Garlic Clove, Minced
  • 4 tbsp of Dried Breadcrumbs
  • 1 tsp of Dried Sage
  • 50g of Medium Cheddar Cheese, Grated

Method:

  1. Heat oven to 200°C/220°C. Wipe the mushrooms to remove any dirt, then turn them upside down and remove the stalks. Place on a baking tray, top-side down.
  2. Fry the bacon lardons for 8-10 minutes until they start to crisp. Meanwhile, finely chop the shallot, mushroom stalks, and garlic.
  3. Add the bacon and fry on moderate heat for approx 5 min until ingredients are cooked.
  4. Take the mixture off the heat and add the breadcrumbs and sage, then tir to make an even mixture.
  5. Spoon mixture into the hollows of the mushrooms and sprinkle with the grated cheese.
  6. Bake for approx 8-10 mins until the cheese has melted. Serve with crusty bread.
